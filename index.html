About

I'm a researcher at the Augmented Vision department of DFKI, working on FPGA/SoC-based acceleration of deep learning and computer vision for real-time, energy-efficient edge applications. My work spans embedded systems, disparity estimation, hand-pose recognition, and architectural optimization for edge-AI deployment.
